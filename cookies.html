Chocolate Chip Cookie Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees Fahrenheit
  2. Cream together the butter and sugars until combined.
  3. Add all the remaining ingredients except for the chocolate chips and mix.
  4. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  5. Place large spoonfuls on a cookie sheet and bake for 10 minutes or until the edges are browned.
